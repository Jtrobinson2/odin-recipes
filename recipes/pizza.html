Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular and versatile dish that originated in Italy but has become a global favorite. It typically consists of a flat, round base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. The dough is often thin and crispy, although thicker crust options are also available. Pizzas are commonly baked in ovens, and they come in a variety of styles, including Neapolitan, New York, Chicago deep-dish, and more. Pizza is enjoyed by people of all ages and is often a social and customizable meal.


Ingredients:

  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Create the dough
  2. Put the sauce on the dough
  3. Put the cheese on the dough
  4. Bake
