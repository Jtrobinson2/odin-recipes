Cookie

Description

A cookie is a small, sweet baked treat, typically round and flat. It consists of basic ingredients like flour, sugar, butter, and eggs, with variations including chocolate chips, nuts, or oats. Cookies come in different textures, from chewy to crunchy, and are baked in an oven. Popular types include chocolate chip, oatmeal, and sugar cookies, enjoyed for their versatility and association with various occasions.
